## ABC Connect App

The ABC Connect app (available on iOS and Android) allows customers to:

- Register products for warranty activation
- Schedule wash/AC cycles remotely
- Monitor appliance status
- Book service requests
- Track service ticket status
- Access digital manuals
- Receive filter/maintenance reminders

Compatible models: All AirSmart ACs, ProWash Combo, and select front-load washer models. Connectivity via Wi-Fi (2.4 GHz).
