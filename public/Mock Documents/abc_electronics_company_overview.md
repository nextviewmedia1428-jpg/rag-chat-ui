# ABC Electronics — Company Overview & Operations

## Company Background

ABC Electronics traces its origins to 1974, when it was established as ABC Manufacturing Limited in collaboration with a Swiss precision-engineering firm. The company began with a focus on fine blanking and precision components before expanding into home appliances in 1989 with the launch of fully automatic washing machines. Today, ABC Electronics is a diversified group with operations across engineering products and consumer home appliances.

Our engineering divisions are based in Kolkata and Bengaluru. The Bengaluru facility is our primary centre for motor manufacturing, serving both white-goods applications and automotive sectors. The appliance business operates across six manufacturing units in India, with facilities in Goa, Rudrapur, Chennai, and other locations.

## Group Structure

Our business is organized into two primary segments:

**Engineering Division** — Covers fine blanking, stamping, precision tooling, motor manufacturing, and aftermarket services. This division serves both internal demand (motors for our own appliance range) and external OEM customers in automotive and industrial sectors.

**Home Appliances Division** — Covers washing machines (front-load, top-load, washer-dryer combinations), air conditioners, refrigerators, dishwashers, microwave ovens, chimneys, hobs, built-in kitchen products, commercial laundry equipment, and commercial dishwashing systems.

## Electronics and Technology Capabilities

Our electronics and technology capabilities span three areas:

**Motor Manufacturing:** Our Bengaluru engineering unit manufactures BLDC (Brushless DC) motors for white-goods applications including front-load and top-load washing machines and air conditioners, as well as motors for automotive applications including engine cooling, battery cooling, and seat ventilation systems. Commercial production of BLDC motors for front-load washing machines commenced in Q3 FY 2024-25.

**Embedded Electronics & Controls:** Across our appliance range, we embed and indigenize critical electronic components. Our air conditioner range includes AI-powered operation, smart-ready controls, geo-sensing technology, hybrid operating modes, and locally manufactured electronics. Our washer-dryer products are integrated with the ABC Connect app for remote operation and monitoring.

**Integrated Electromechanical Solutions:** Our strategic direction is to combine motors, power electronics, and software into complete integrated solutions for OEM customers. This capability — built first for internal use in appliances — is now being extended to external customers.

## Technology Direction

Our near-term technology priorities are:

1. **BLDC motor rollout:** Extending BLDC motors across more appliance categories, including ACs, after the front-load washer programme.
2. **Electronics indigenization:** Reducing import dependence for AC electronics and motors under the Make in India initiative.
3. **Connected appliances:** Expanding ABC Connect app integration and smart-ready product architecture.
4. **OEM motor supply:** Using proven internal motor capability to serve external OEM customers and better utilize Bengaluru capacity.
5. **Commercial appliance growth:** Targeting 3x revenue growth in commercial laundry over three years; pursuing institutional, government, and export markets.

## Competitive Position

We hold the second position in the microwave oven market in India. In dishwashers, we are among the top three brands by market share. Our AC market share target is to grow from approximately 3% to 5% in FY 2025-26, supported by new models, AI features, and indigenized components.

Our competitive strengths lie in localized technology, service network depth, premium brand positioning in laundry and kitchen products, and our unique position as the only major appliance brand with in-house BLDC motor manufacturing capability.

## Strategic Priorities

- Triple engineering business revenue through new orders, M&A, and external OEM wins
- Improve appliance margins through a ₹200+ crore cost reduction programme (ongoing with external consultants)
- Accelerate commercial appliance growth in institutions, government, and exports
- Deepen electronics localization across all appliance categories
- Launch integrated motors-power electronics-software solutions for external OEM market
